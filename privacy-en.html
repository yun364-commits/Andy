BottleCalendar Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

We do not collect your data

BottleCalendar does not create accounts, track users, use ads, or send product names, photos, dates, prices, or notes to a server.

Local product data

Product names, brands, categories, opened dates, PAO months, optional fixed expiry dates, prices, notes, photos, status, and timestamps are stored locally on your device by default.

Photos

The app uses Apple PhotosPicker. You choose specific photos, and the app does not request full photo library access. Selected photos are copied into local app storage only when you save a product.

Notifications

Reminders are generated and handled locally by the device. Notification permission is optional, and the app remains usable if permission is denied.

Purchases

Pro purchase and restore are handled by Apple StoreKit. The app reads purchase entitlement status to unlock Pro features.

Backups

JSON backup export and import are started by the user. Backup files may contain product records and photo data, so users should store them carefully.

Contact

To contact the developer, email houyun2020@163.com.

Suggested check dates

Suggested check dates are only for organization and reminders. They are not medical, safety, or product-spoilage conclusions. Please judge products using their label, smell, color, texture, and brand instructions.
